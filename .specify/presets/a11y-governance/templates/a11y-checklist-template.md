## Perceivable

- Text alternatives provided for non-decorative images and diagrams
  (1.1.1):
- Information and relationships conveyed by structure are programmatically
  determinable (1.3.1):

- Contrast meets `4.5:1` for normal text and `3:1` for large text
  (1.4.3):
